正文：首先感谢坛友分享，Highland Park属于有口碑也有价格的酒厂，OB的重雪莉之前论坛有大佬分享，可惜未能开成，无缘尝试，这只刚拿了旧金山烈酒比赛双金，开心的发现手里有分享，美滋滋的来波酒评。
Bottler：Scotch Malt Whisky Society
Bottling serie：Volcanic Jam
Vintage：20 Aug 2001
Bottled：29 May 2017
Casktype：Refill Ex-Sherry Butt
Casknumber：4.229
Strength：60.3 % Vol.
Appearance：亮金色
Nose：成熟至果皮破裂的多汁水果，杏子、李子、樱桃、车厘子果酱，黄糖，很甜的蜂蜜，石楠，香草，极轻微的烟火气。
Boby：饱满，较油润，些许辛辣。
Palate：果酱，橙皮，柠檬，蜂蜜，焦糖，甘草，橡木，中药，胡椒，草木灰，黑巧，烤焦的咖啡豆。
Finish：中等，橡木，坚果，草药。
没啥雷，值这个价吧。话说SMWS的品鉴委员会是真的厉害，读品鉴词的时候常常一脸懵逼，自己动手写完之后发现还是要服气的。
SMWS tasting notes: The nose was a volcanic eruption of sweet heat like raspberry jam gurgling and bubbling on the stove. Puffs of smoke carried the alluring aromas of burning applewood and roast duck with honey-coated vegetables. A complex floral character was definitely present like heather and bog myrtle but also with lavender, thyme and rosemary. The palate continued along a path of roasted meat, perhaps venison or pork with devilishly tempting juicy crackling that had been singed to charcoal along its edges. Heavy liquorice and star anise combined with salted caramel and the dark berries of rich fruitcake but also with the citric tang of fresh grapefruit. The finish merged smoky and ashy wood with earthy herbs and the suggestion of chocolate sauce.
