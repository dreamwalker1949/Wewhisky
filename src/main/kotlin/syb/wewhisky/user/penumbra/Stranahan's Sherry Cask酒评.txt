标题：Stranahan's Sherry Cask酒评
链接：http://wewhisky.com/forum.php?mod=viewthread&tid=11507
发布时间：2018-12-02 22:57
正文：感谢@世间一双眼 的分享。
Mash Bill：100％Malted BarleyStated Age：4Bottled：2017Casktype：New Charred Oak + Oloroso Sherry（First Fill＆Refill）Batch：？Strength：47.0% Vol.Appearance：铁锈色。
Aroma：远闻有干邑感，榛子巧克力布朗尼，葡萄干，深色水果果干，咖啡豆，香草，茴香，肉桂，焦糖，橡木，清漆。
在苏格兰传统重雪莉的基础上多了类似干邑的水果味与较为丰富的香草与蛋糕。
Boby：较强，偏酸，中等油脂。
Palate：苦杏仁，草药酒，黑巧，烤榛子，不甜的深色水果果干，枫糖，雪莉，做旧的皮革，肉桂，姜粉，橡木，些微单宁、盐分与香草。
Finish：极长久，轻微灼烧感，雪莉酒，些微的甜度下是长久的黑巧，木浆，肉桂，少量单宁。
桶形详细信息：
Stranahan’s Sherry Cask is our latest innovation & expression in single malt. We take our four-year-old Rocky Mountain Single Malt and then transfer it to 500-liter Oloroso Sherry Barrels to cask finish. Procured from the Andalusia region of southern Spain, these sherry barrels have been aging wine for over 40 years, leaving a depth of flavor soaked into the ancient oak staves.
