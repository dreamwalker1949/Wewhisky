标题：Ben Nevis 1997 SMWS 78.41(重雪莉之卅一)
链接：http://wewhisky.com/forum.php?mod=viewthread&tid=11919
发布时间：2018-12-19 22:44
正文：感谢@hiori 的分享。
Bottler：Scotch Malt Whisky Society
Bottling serie：A real sherry monster
Vintage：31 Dec 1997
Bottled：16 Nov 2017
Casktype：Refill Ex-Oloroso Sherry Butt
Casknumber：78.41
Outturn：726
Strength：57.1% Vol.
Appearance：深琥珀色。
Aroma：红枣，皮革，臭鳜鱼，李子，杏脯，葡萄干，蜜糖，果酱，蜂蜡，烟熏干花，蛋糕，椰浆，金属，硝石，海盐，胡椒，酸橙，香草，无花果。富有冲击力而平衡的雪莉桶特征，构成了这支酒的绝对主线，虽然如此，并没有在复杂度上失分，酸甜、咸甜、干湿各个方面达到了绝佳的平衡。
Boby：饱满，油润。
Palate：海盐，甜面酱，坚果巧克力酱，姜丝，胡椒，油醋汁，橙皮，葡萄柚，焦糖芝士慕斯，烤坚果，蜜饯，咖啡，皮革，烟丝，腐殖质。
Finish：中长，烟火余烬，SO2，姜丝，坚果，咖啡，Amontillado。
性价比极致之选！欠缺的只是1%的天赋或是灵气吧。
Notes from the website: https://www.smws.com/a-real-sherry-monsterThe nose took us to straight sherry-land – raisins, figs, fruit cake, cinder toffee, custard flan, sweet pipe tobacco, almond biscuits and Madeiran molasses cake – a resonating cello of a dram. The neat palate was a real sherry monster – treacle toffee, PX, muscovado, liquorice, Eccles cakes and tongue-etching coal tar – fire-breathing dragons arrived from the bodegas of Jerez. With water however, the nose became syrupy sweet – crème brûlée crust, caramel wafers, bramble cheesecake and date loaf – actually a pleasure in its own right. The palate became candy sweet – tablet, chocolate brownie and toffee; an after-tingle of ginger cake – now a sheer delight.
