标题：Knockando 1996 Managers' Choice(重雪莉横评之五)
链接：http://wewhisky.com/forum.php?mod=viewthread&tid=5941
发布时间：2018-03-03 03:30
正文：可以说是意外之喜吧，之前从没留意过这支酒，由于bartender的推荐选择的这只，感觉拓宽了对重雪莉的边界的理解。摘录一段评论：洛坎多威士忌气味非常高雅，香气四溢让人有身处花园中的错觉。口感十分柔顺怡人，甜美的风味当中释放出各种果味，蜜味，奶油味及隐约的泥煤味。
Bottling serie：The Managers' ChoiceVintage：11.07.1996
Bottled：18.03.2009
Casktype：Spanish Sherry European OakStrength：58.5%Vol.Appearance：胡桃木
Nose：果酸，雪莉，熟车厘子，麦梗，肉桂。
Boby：强，中等油润，较柔顺。
Mouth：低辛辣，熟水果，莓果，温柔的火堆，摩卡，低盐分，些许话梅，车厘子，水果混香。Finish：中偏长，温热，一丝丝甜，雪莉。
特别温柔的雪莉炸弹，味道纯粹干净，温暖而肉质丰厚，重雪莉中最speyside的一只。

penumbra：2012 Whisky Bible
Nose:I think my staff have poured me the wrong stuff: theyhave opened up some pot still Demerara, right ...? Wrong. No definitely from the Knockando sample. Well, Well, Well, let`s just say it is a fruity, sugary, rich start on the nose.
Key:Actually, I don`t care if this is rum or whiskey. When you get this beautifully defined flavors, this is a body sublimely structure ... it really does not much matter. Oozes fruity, beautifully natural sweetened quality and complexity.
Finish:A slight bitterness on the finish where, for the first time, there is a minor blemish, but the array of sugars quiet make for something memorable ... and absolutely 100% nothing like I have tasted from any previous Knockando.
Balance:I have been in the Demerara region of Guyana, crawled around a rum warehouse at a rum distillery and opened a cask of rum Which has less rum characteristics than this rumbustuous chappie. Quite a rum DO.
